#### **We are looking for a Chinese Noodle and Snack and Hot Drink stall to operate in our school canteen .**


#### **Application Procedure**

#### **Step 1: Application Form**
* Download and complete the application form [here](/files/wsps_canteen_application_form.pdf).<br>
* You may also request for a hardcopy from the School’s General Office. 

#### **Step 2: Required supporting documents to submit together with the application form**<br>

•	Photocopy of NRIC (front and back)<br>
•	Proposed menu list with pricing for all items<br>
•	Photocopy of relevant certificates (e.g. Basic Food Hygiene Course, etc.)

#### **Step 3: Application and supporting document submission**<br>

Submit the completed application form and supporting documents by the closing date via email or in person to the School’s General Office.<br>

•	School Address: 2 Pasir Ris Street 11, Singapore 519075<br>
•	Contact No: 6922 9100<br>
•	Email: wsps@moe.edu.sg<br>
•	Contact Persons: Mr Roy Tay<br>

**How to get here:**<br>
•	Nearest MRT: Pasir Ris MRT<br>
•	Bus Number: 12, 17, 21, 39, 53, 81, 109, 359, 518



#### **Eligibility and Criteria**
•	Singapore Citizen or Singapore Permanent Resident (SPR) for both stallholder and stall assistant(s)<br>
•	Able to provide good service and quality food at reasonable prices, determined in consultation with the school<br>
•	Food preparation must adhere to Health Promotion Board (HPB) “Healthy Meals in Schools Programme (HMSP)” criteria (refer to this link here: Healthy Meals in Schools Programme )<br>
•	Able to observe high standard of food and personal hygiene in compliance to Singapore Food Agency (SFA) and National Environment Agency (NEA) practices and guidelines.<br>
•	Healthy and fit to operate the stall<br>
•	Must be in personal attendance during stall operating hours<br>
•	Must maintain stall cleanliness at all times<br>
•	Be onboard GIRO for payment to school.<br>
•	Willing to bill school through vendors@gov (for small quantity order).<br>
•	Willing to onboard the POSB SmartBuddy Programme.<br>
•	Successful applicant and stall assistance(s) must possess the WSQ Basic Food Hygiene Certificate.<br>
•	Successful applicant will need to apply for a food stall license from Singapore Food Agency (SFA).<br>
•	Shortlisted applicants will be notified for an interview and required to provide food sampling for the School Canteen Selection Committee at the applicant’s own expense
