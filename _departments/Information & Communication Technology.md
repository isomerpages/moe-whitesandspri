### **Information &amp; Communication Technology**
#### **Objectives**
The ICT department endeavor to create a conducive and enriched learning environment for teachers and students to leverage ICT in teaching and learning. 

#### **Key Programmes**
##### **ICT in Teaching and Learning**
ICT is integrated into the curriculum to allow our pupils to experience quality learning empowered with technology. Teachers make use of a variety of pedagogical practices such as Ed-Tech Practices to leverage on digital technologies to deepen studentsâ€™ understanding and learning.

##### **Student-Initiated Learning**
The Student-Initiated Learning (SIL) programme is conducted to nurture our students into passionate learners. SIL aims to develop student agency and promote lifelong learning through providing students dedicated time and space for them to pursue their interests and passions in areas such as Art, Active Lifestyle, Mass Communication and many more.
